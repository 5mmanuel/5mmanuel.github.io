Below some infomation about cyberbullying

Learn about Cyber Stalking
  • Cyberstalking is persistent and intrusive online harassment.
  • Involves repeated communication and monitoring of online activities.
  • Includes false impersonation, threats, intimidation, harassment, doxxing, and manipulation.
  • Aims to control, cause distress, or harm victims through digital platforms.
  • Can have severe emotional and psychological consequences.
  • Legal measures vary by jurisdiction; victims are advised to report incidents and safeguard their online presence.
ostracism

  • Ostracism Definition: Social exclusion or intentional isolation of an individual.
  • Actions Involved: Withholding social acceptance, interaction, or inclusion.
  • Spreading rumors about you online.
  • Hacking into your social media accounts or email.
  • Making fun of you or calling you names online.
  • Consequences: Leads to feelings of isolation, marginalization, and negative emotions.
  • Occurrence: Can happen in various social settings, both offline and online.
  • Impacts: Significantly affects mental well-being and the sense of belonging.
Learn about Online Harassment

  • Sharing someone's home address online without their permission.
  • Posting someone's phone number online and encouraging others to call or text them.
  • Sharing someone's social media accounts online and encouraging others to harass them.
  • Posting someone's personal information on a website or forum.

Trickery

Trickery

  • Definition: Involves deceptive actions or manipulation to mislead or harm an individual.
  • Example: Creating fake profiles, spreading false rumors, or using deceitful tactics to exploit or embarrass the victim.
Outing

  • Definition: Revealing private or sensitive information about an individual without their consent.
  • Example: Disclosing details like sexual orientation or personal relationships without permission.

Flaming

  • Definition: Posting offensive or inflammatory messages online.
  • Platform: Common in discussion forums, social media, and digital spaces.
  • Behavior: Involves hostile and aggressive communication.
  • Intent: Aimed at inciting emotional reactions and escalating conflicts.
  • Characteristics: Includes personal attacks, insults, and offensive language.
  • Impact: Can negatively affect targeted individuals and the overall tone of online discussions.
